Little Lemon


Chicago

Little Lemon is owned by two Italian brothers, Mario and Adrian, who moved to the United States to pursue their shared dream of owning a restaurant. Mario relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef in Italy. Adrian does all the marketing for the restaurant and led the effort to expand the menu beyond classic Italian to incorporate additional cuisines from the Mediterranean region.




Little Lemon Menu

Explore our menu with different Italian dishes from the Mediterranean region.

A Fragrant herbs hand-picked from sun-drenched gardens, simmering into rich sauces that coat perfectly al dente pasta. Fresh, seasonal vegetables burst with flavor, complementing succulent cuts of meat prepared with traditional techniques. Every dish whispers of our Nonna's kitchen, where laughter mingled with the intoxicating aromas of Italy.

At Little Lemon, we believe in the power of food to connect people. Our recipes are more than just ingredients; they're stories waiting to be shared. With every bite, you'll taste the passion, the tradition, and the love that goes into every dish.

So, come experience the magic of Little Lemon. Let us tantalize your taste buds and transport you to the heart of Italy, one delicious mouthful at a time.
